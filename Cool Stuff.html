How Monkey Kart Works

  1. How it Works
    • Show your international drivers license.
    • Pick one of many fun costumes! (I was Snorlax from Pokemon)
    • Follow your guide around Tokyo and they'll take pictures along the way.

  2. Where You Drive
    • Shinjuku - Tokyo's Main Business and Nightlife Core
    • Harajuku - The Mecca of Japanese Youth Fashion and Trends
    • Shibuya - Iconic Shopping District with World's Busiest Pedestrian Crossing
  3. Why I Loved It!
    • Literally driving around the streets of freakin' Tokyo!
    • Countless people waved and took pics of me in my Snorlax outfit ^__^
    • Our fun tour guide, Ken, took many memorable photos of us.
